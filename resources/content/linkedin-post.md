## Version EN

Just read Anthropic's announcement and... I'm honestly stunned.

Starting May 1st, Claude is ditching subscriptions for a new usage-based model called "Fisher Pricing".

New rates:
- Input tokens: $0.30 to $0.90 / 1K tokens
- Output tokens: $1.50 to $4.50 / 1K tokens
- Monthly minimum: $350

Here's the painful math: typical usage (~100 messages/day) would go from $20/month... to roughly $4,950/month.

Yes. You read that right. x247.

They say infrastructure costs went up 340% this year. Sure. But we're talking about a 250x increase for users.

I get that they need to be profitable, but did we just lose one of our best tools?
